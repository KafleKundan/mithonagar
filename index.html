About Us

Science and technology have taken a giant leap in this century. Technology has brought everything at our fingertips, but there was always a thing it lacked.As a foodie i used to quote" Technology is of no use when we couln't download food".But, what if we could ? This thing drived me and my colleges to make something that was as convinient as downloading food. This curiousity and enthusiasm of ours lead us to create "MithoNagar".
Mithonagar is a leading brand for online food delivery service of Biratnagar since 3 years. Our team of highly motivated and skillful members provide you service 24/7. We value our customers convinence more than anything. You just wish for the food from your favourite restaurant and we get it ready on you door-step within few minutes.
Here is something that separates us from our competitors and why people choose us over them.
